Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, October 2, 2006
6:30 P.M.
Hare: Great at Giving Head
Location: Great Swamp, West Kingston, RI

 

Directions:

1. From I-95 North or South, take Exit 3A (Rt. 138 East).
2. Follow Rt. 138 East past the intersection of Rt. 2.
3. Within roughly 1 mile after crossing Rt. 2, Rt. 138 will go over the train tracks.
4. Watch for the Liberty Lane turn off to the South within about 0.5 miles of the train tracks.
5. Follow Liberty Lane until it dead ends at the train tracks.
6. Turn left onto the dirt road (Great Neck Road).
7. Follow Great Neck Road past the park headquarters on your right.
8. Park when you come to a parking area terminated by a gate across Great Neck.
9. Look for hashers.

RIH3 Dragon Boat Race - 2005
Saturday, September 9, 2006
8:00 A.M.
Hare: Dr. WHO
Location: Pawtucket, RI

 

Di-erections:
(From the South)

  1. 95 North to Exit 28
  2. Turn R at the end of the ramp onto School Street
  3. Pass the entrance to the Dragon Boat Area (Tim Healey Way) and Find the Medical Bldg (333 School Street) on your Right
  4. Park at the far end, and walk towards the river and the smell of rotting fish. Find trail to the Right.

Di-erections:
(From the North)

  1. 95 South to Exit 27
  2. Take a left at the end of the ramp, and after the double lights, left again.
  3. Pass Mr USA Cleaners, Cross the river on the Division Street Bridge, and turn Right on School Street after the vacant lot.
  4. Follow the above for parking.

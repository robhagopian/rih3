Directions:

From Providence and North (This is the shortest route, and best if there's traffic on 95):

Rt. 6 West to Danielson Pike (Left fork, soon after Bishop Hill Tavern, before 101)
Go about 2 miles and, after reservoir and State Police, turn Left on Rockland Road
After less than 4 miles, there is an intersection. Continue Straight on Rts. 102/14
After about 5 miles, turn Right on 14 as they split.
Go about 4.5 miles on 14 (0.9 miles after passing Rt. 117) and turn Left on dirt Road with brown DEM sign for Carbuncle Pond Fishing Area.
Park in lot near the handicapped access(!) dock.

From South of Providence (or if there's no traffic):

95 South to exit 6, not 6A
North on Rt. 3, past Rosies, just over a mile to light. Turn Left on Harkney Hill Road (Rt. 118).
About 5.5 miles on Harkney Hill west to 102. Turn Right (north).
After less than a mile, turn Left on 117.
After 2.8 miles at Stop sign and Hopkins Hill Road, turn Right, continuing on Rt. 117
After less than 2 miles, turn Left on Rt. 14.
After 0.9 miles, look for the DEM sign, and turn Left onto dirt road.

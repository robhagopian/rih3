Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, June 5, 2006
6:30 P.M.
Hare: Great at Giving Head
Location: Arcadia, RI

 

Directions:

1. Take I-95 to Exit 5, then take Rt. 102 East to Rt. 3.

2. Take Rt. 3 South to the intersection of Rt. 3 and Rt. 165.

3. From the intersection, take Rt. 165 for 1.4 miles West to Arcadia Rd. (not marked as Arcadia Rd.) on the left (South).

4. Look for the signs on both sides of the road for Arcadia management area.

5. Follow the Arcadia Rd. South for about 1-1.5 miles till you reach Browning Mill Pond parking on your right (West).

6. Look for hashers.

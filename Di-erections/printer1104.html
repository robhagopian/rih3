Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, May 28, 2006
2:00 P.M.
Hare: Async
Location: Douglas, MA

 

Di-erections:

  1. Take 146 north to the Forestdale-Slatersville exit.
  2. At the end of the ramp, turn right, then immediately left onto Route 146A.
  3. Follow 146A north for about one mile to the intersection of Routes 5, 146A, & 102.
  4. Proceed straight onto Route 102 for just over 4 miles and turn right onto Route 107.
  5. Follow Route 107 north for about 3-1/2 miles to Route 100 in the village of Pascoag.
  6. Follow Route 100 north for 3.2 miles to Buck Hill Road (look for “Route 12 Connecticut” sign on right).
  7. Turn left on Buck Hill Road and follow for 3 miles to the end.
  8. Turn right on Quaddick Town Farm Road (sign missing!)
  9. Follow Quaddick Town Farm Road for 0.6 miles to New Road.
  10. Turn right on New Road and follow 0.8 miles to the end.
  11. Turn right on East Thompson Road (sign missing!)
  12. Proceed 1.1 miles on East Thompson Road to the start at Gate #19, opposite High Road.
  13. Park on the side of the road, and apply sun screen, tick repellent and Vaseline.

Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, October 27, 2007
6:30 P.M.
Hare: Amish It Head
Location: Melville, RI

 

Directions from the North:
1. Take 195E to 24S
2. Continue on 24S until it turns into 114S/West Main Rd
3. Continue on 114S/West Main Rd through 3 stoplights
4. At 4th stoplight turn right onto Stringham Rd (at the Dunkin' Donuts)
5. Follow Stringham Rd just under 0.5mile, turn right onto Sullivan Dr
6. Follow Sullivan Dr until it dead ends (~0.5mile), turn left on Chelsea Dr
7. Pass the campgrounds and trailered boats on your right and park in front of the large yellowish abandoned building

Directions from the South
1. Take 138E over the bridges (Oh NO!!11!! Over the bridges!!!!!!1!!11)
2. Continue on 138E, make a left onto 114N/West Main Rd
3. Drive a bunch - through all those crappy stop lights and past all those fancy stores
4. Eventually you'll go downhill (and then uphill again) past the Lawton Valley Reservoir - just past the uphill you'll see the Dunkin' Donuts
5. Use above directions - good luck

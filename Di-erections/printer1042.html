Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, March 20, 2005
6:30 P.M.
Hare: Basket Boom Boom
Location: Cumberland, RI

 

Directions:

Take 123 east off Rte 146 to Broad Street and go right about an eighth mile on the left to St Patrick’s.

    Or

Take 295 to Mendon Rd and go east to the traffic lights at the junction with Broad St and follow left on Broad 2 or 3 sets of lights. After crossing Rte 123 go left into St. Patrick’s.

    Or

From 95 take exit 30, (southbound: Roosevelt Avenue is the first exit in RI and northbound: East ST.is the last exit in RI) go west off exit and take first legal right on Branch at the sharp left turn. Follow Branch along the Blackstone River through construction and go left after barriers. Continue on Branch which turns into Mill St after rail tracks. At end go right on Broad St. to St. Patrick’s about a half mile on the right.

    Or

Look up St. Patrick’s at 301 Broad Street, Cumberland, RI

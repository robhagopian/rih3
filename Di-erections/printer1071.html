Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, October 9, 2006
6:30 P.M.
Hare: Dry Foot Fairy
Location: West Cranston, RI

 

Directions:

1. From I-295, take Exit 4 for Plainfield Pike (Rt. 14) and head WEST on Plainfield Pike, then turn LEFT onto Comstock Parkway.
(For those heading South on I-295, make a Right at the bottom of the exit ramp. Comstock Pkwy will be the First stoplight. For those heading North on I-295, make a Left at the end of exit the ramp. Comstock Pkwy will be the Second stoplight.)
2. Take Comstock Pkwy to the next stoplight (Scituate Ave). Make a RIGHT onto Scituate Ave (Rt. 12) heading West again.
3. On Scituate Ave., go about 2.4 miles or so to a blinking orange light (Seven Mile Rd).
4. Make a RIGHT at the blinking orange light onto Seven Mile Rd, and make an immediate LEFT into the church parking lot.
5. Park in the Upper lot, where you see the basketball hoop. 6:30 HST.

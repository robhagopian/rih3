Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, May 29, 2006
2:00 P.M.
Hare: Basket Boom Boom
Location: Harrisville, RI

 

Directions:

From 146 take the Forestdale/Slatersville exit and go right off exit and left onto Great Rd, 146A north.

Continue straight through a few intersections and Great Road turns into Victory Highway Rte 102 south. Continue south past the Burrillville Middle High on right at top of hill.

To get to Basket’s, make next left at Bella’s Restaurant, take a right and first left onto Snake Hill Rd. Don’t go over bridge.

To go directly to the start, continue past Bella’s and take next right on East Ave Rte 107. Follow East Ave past the now famous Burrillville High and cross the Clear River Bridge.

Then take an immediate right into the Assembly Parking lot.

Parking may be tight, so additional parking can be found across the street at the “Ol Stillwater Mill” complex.

To keep costs to a minimum, this will once again be Pot Luck. Bring a dish and save 5 bucks.

Oh yea, there will be beer.

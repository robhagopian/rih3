Happy New Year!

The hash is starting from Manville Memorial Park, North Smithfield at 4 pm New Years Day. This will be an A to B, and Bondo, our hare, suggests arriving at his house for a beer by 3:30, then ride over with him. If driving directly to the park the directions are: from 146 take Rte 99 to first exit and go east on Sayles Hill Rd, or if you miss the exit continue north on 146 and at traffic signal go east on Sayles Hill towards Manville. Continue east on Sayles Hill Rd to right turn at intersection with Old River Road on left (Rte 126). Take an immediate right turn into park at intersection.

Directions to Bondo's house, continue north on 146 to the Forestdale & Slatersville exit and go right off ramp and right at the lights. Continue south on 146a to next traffic signal and left down St Paul Street. Continue down hill and over bridge. At T intersection, go right on Harris Ave (Rte 122) for about a half mile. Go past Cold Spring park on right and take next left on Meadow Rd. Bondo's house is a block and a half up Meadow on right at 157 Meadow Rd. His phone number is 401-766-9447 if you get lost.

For those that want to start the year off with a splash, join us at Mackerel Cove in Jamestown, RI for a dip with the Penguin's at noon. Call me for a ride or directions. Then we'll ride up to the hash.

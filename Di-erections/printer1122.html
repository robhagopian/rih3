Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, October 1, 2007
6:30 P.M.
Hare: Bondo Jovi
Location: Woonsocket, RI

 

Directions:

1. Take Route 146 North to the Forestdale/Slatersville exit. (It’s the last exit in RI on northbound 146).

2. At the end of the ramp, turn right, then right again onto Route 146A South.

3. Follow Route 146A south for about ¼ mile to the bottom of the hill and turn left at the light onto St. Paul Street.

4. Follow St Paul Street about 1.5 miles to the end in Blackstone, MA.

5. Turn right on Main Street.

6. Follow Main Street back into Rhode Island. (The street becomes Harris Ave in RI)

7. Shortly after passing Cold Spring Park on the right, turn left onto Meadow Road.

8. About two long blocks or so, on the right, is Bondo’s abode at #157 Meadow.

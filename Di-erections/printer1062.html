Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, August 7, 2006
6:30 P.M.
Hare: Amish It Head
Location: Portsmouth, RI

 

Directions:

From Providence/North:
1. Take 195E through Fall River
2. Take Exit 8A just after the Fall River Bridge onto 24S
3. 24S will narrow down and turn into 114S/West Main Rd
4. Once on 114S/West Main Rd continue ~3miles and turn left onto Union St at a light (there will be a sign on the left at this intersection for a plant nursery)
5. Continue on Union St ~.25mile and then turn right onto Jepson Ln.
6. Continue on Jepson Ln for another ~.25mile and school will be on the right. Park in the first lot you see.

From the South:
1. Do what you need to do to get to the bridges to Newport - go over both the Jamestown and Newport-Pell Bridge ($2 each way) on 138E
2. After the Newport-Pell bridge, veer to the left continuing on 138E to 114N (Fall River/Cape Cod direction)
3. This road dead ends into Admiral Kalbfus Rd, make a left at the light continuing on 138E.
4. At the third light make a left onto West Main Rd/138E.
5. Continue straight on 138E (will turn into 114N) for ~3.7 miles
6. Turn right onto Union St, continue ~.25 mile and then turn right onto Jepson Ln.
7. Continue on Jepson Ln for another ~.25mile and school will be on the right. Park in the first lot you see.

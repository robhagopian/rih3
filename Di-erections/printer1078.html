Rhode Island Hash House Harriers
Monday, November 27, 2006
6:30 P.M.
Hare: Trail Hoover
Location: Sharon, MA

 

Directions:

- RT 95 North to Exit 10 in Sharon, MA

- Follow RT 27 South (right if coming from RI, left if coming from Boston) for a few miles, over the railroad tracks (bridge) to a pair of lights in Sharon Center.

- Go straight through both lights and follow 0.9 miles to East St.

- Take a RIGHT onto East St and follow 0.8 miles to Mountain Rd (just after a graveyard on left)

- Take a LEFT onto Mountain Rd and follow for a few miles. The paved road will turn to dirt for a short time. Just as it turns back to pavement again, pull over and park on the right shoulder. Park only on the dirt road, no parking allowed on the paved road.

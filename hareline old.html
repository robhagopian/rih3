 Misdirection's:

Go to Map Quest and search for Beavertail State Park, RI

 D'erections:

Take I-95 south or I-295 south towards Warwick,

Take Rt. 4 south off I-95 which will fork left because this is Rhode Island,

Continue south until Rt. 4 merges with Rt. 1 south and bare right,

Continue south and take Exit for Rt. 138 east for Newport/Jamestown,

After crossing bridge immediately take first exit and continue straight for North Main Rd,

Turn right onto N. Main Rd and continue straight for many miles,

Pass Mackerel Cove Beach on left,

Travel approximately 3 miles to Beavertail,

Park in first large parking lot on right.

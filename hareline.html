Alright you animals, forty years in and we’re still getting together for a little liquid bonding.
Pay as you go bar hopping. You handle your own tab, we handle making sure you don’t go home alone with your dignity intact. We’ll wander from bar to bar like a well practiced bad habit, raising glasses and lowering standards in honor of four decades of dirty jokes and even dirtier friendships.
Expect tight groups, loose morals, and plenty of opportunities to squeeze into a photo you might need to crop later. Lots of cheering, a little jeering, and just enough poor decisions to keep things interesting.
Come celebrate the kind of camaraderie that only comes from shared laughs, shared drinks, and absolutely no shame about either.

The Very Hairy Cinco de Mustache' Hash
And the RIH3's 40th Analversary

Monday May 4th. The big Schwartz. RIH3 turns 40 and somehow still hasn’t learned a damn thing. We’re kicking off from a “charmingly inadequate” little parking lot in Foster, which means carpool, or prepare to park in someone’s front yard.

Trail’s a tight little 2.5 miles on papier, which in hash math means long enough to question your life choices but short enough to make it back before total moral collapse. Expect ticks with commitment issues, ankle-snapping boulders, water crossings that look shallow until they’re not, and terrain that really wants to see you fall down in front of your friends.

It’s also the annual Mustache Hash, so bring your best lip sweater. Real, fake, drawn on, glued on, or spiritually implied. The worse it looks, the better it drinks. Bonus points if it holds foam.

This is 40 years of bad decisions, good friends, and proudly low standards. Come celebrate the legacy: getting lost on purpose, finding beer eventually, and laughing at whoever can’t sing the melodies.

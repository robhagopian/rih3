Run # 1392 – December 3rd 2012<br>

Start: Fort Adam’s, Newport<br>

Hare: AssQuack and Crabby Shag<br>

Weather: Mild<br>

Pack: Basket, Rusty, Wee Balls, She Male, Just Mark, Oozie, DogMeat, Pubic Housing, Krisco Kid, Pussy Galore, WHO<br>

Visitors: (Overseas) Snotty<br>

Visitors: (Stateside) Just Eric from Groton CT<br><br>

 

Fresh from a honeymoon in exotic Thailand, & free of all known communicable diseases we were fortunate to have clear skies (shooting star seen again), & very mild weather for the return of our International duo. But, we were not there for the weather, instructions minimal and so was the pack. Rumor had it that Basket was going to cum with Snotty tonight but there was no sign of either by 6:31pm. <br><br>

 

When you head out towards the point of a peninsula you eventually have to come back, Just Mark was making headway along trail as was SheMail followed by the rare sighting of Krisco Kid. Meantime, Rusty knew the lay of the land and headed over the rugby pitch to higher ground in the accompaniment of Wee Balls. They intersected the pack as they were running back up the hill towards the military housing. Pubic and Donkey admired said housing and wondered about the potential for a name change, the conclusion was that the RIH3 would hardly agree to such a radical change so the idea was hastily dropped. <br><br>

 

Oozie reached the check at the top of the hill, Dr. WHO surveyed the situation &, as usual he sniffed out the route without even looking for flour. We were now on the west side of the fort and the bay glistened ahead with Jamestown a mere stones throw on the other side. <br><br>

 

A check near the water proved to be the most difficult encounter of the evening, there were many routes to choose from but Pussy Galore led most of us into the bushes for what Snotty would describe as a “smashing time” when we recanted the story to him later in the evening. Through those bushes was a hidden building likely used by the former NH3 for firing cannonballs at the Boston H3 as they tried to invade Newport back in 1969. Meanwhile, the smart one’s simply went around the side of the brush & found trail. <br><br>

 

A short stroll up & over the hill brought us within sight of the cars and a check that was looked upon as if it were a warm Bud Lite. At the same time a car was seen trolling the parking lot, it pulled over and the driver appeared to ask questions about pricing, location, size, you get the picture. We soon found out that it was Basket& he was happy to note that the pack was under the impression that the run was done, the time was only 7:02pm & we had only covered a mile and a half. Snotty & DogMeat were disappointed as they had been looking forward to an absolutely fabulous time. <br><br>

 

The hares strolled in and joined those mulling about, they directed us across the dark street and said that the trail went that way and sure enough we were soon on beer. Mighty songs were sung, and they reverberated across the silent harbor giving that ghostly feel of pirates returning to the area. Shortly after the beer stop we were, once again, told in true RIH3 tradition that we should go back hence we came & amass across the street for the circle. <br><br>

 

This we did, and in between a bunch of garages that appeared to be a popular knocking spot for locals. No fewer than 3 cars turned in towards us, only to be followed by the grinding noise of a car being put swiftly into reverse. The mind wanders as to whom these dignitaries or socialites might be, had they no homes to go to – or perhaps the wife was waiting there. <br><br>

 

Snotty was acknowledged for having travelled over the most bridges to get to Newport, Krisco had been missing for at least 3 months, Just Mark hadn’t been around in a while and our CT visitor Just Eric didn’t know any jokes. <br><br>

 

As usual, the pack tried to give Rusty (the holder of the hash shit) the hash shit again tonight because he left the circle during formalities. However, at that very moment Dr. WHO was absent from the circle as he relieved himself against a nearby electric fence. WHO soon became the wearer of his very own creation – the Douche Bag. <br><br>

 

We retired to KJ’s in Middletown, we tried to get AssQuack’s mug but the barkeep was having nun of it. WHO collected dollars and made DogMeat dance on the table. We all promised to return the following week. <br>

Pulaski Park Shiggy Romp

Run #925 January 5, 2004

Hare: Async
Location: Pulaski Recreation Area, Foster/Burrillville
Weather: Mid 30's, continuous drizzle
Present: WIPOS, Dr WHO, Basket Boom Boom, Oozing SD, SESYB, Hopeless, Fuwangi Boner.

The Run: 

A counterclockwise loop, beginning at the turn off for the parking area, and heading northeast on a gas pipeline which led to Center Trail. The fine weather led to fine icing of the ground, the bushes and leaves; but still was warm enough to provide fine swamp shiggy underfoot.

The trail led north off the Center trail into the woods, leading to the first of several major and minor crossings of Keach Brook and its branches.

The Inner Border trail was reached and followed to the covered bridge, crossed east and then taking a completely unnecessary shiggy romp up the North South Trail, into the woods through more excellent wetness and back to the covered bridge. The hare's a wank!

Back west on the Inner Border Trail then south into the woods to the Hemlock Glen Trail, and the BC, near Peck Pond. No one lost at any time. Pathetic.

On out to the pond, then south out via the warm-up hut and the parking area and back to Pulaski Road and the cars.

Ratings: 6.9: No Bondo, fine weather.

Hashit: WHO, No Hopeless!

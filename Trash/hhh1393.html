Run # 1393 – December 10th 2012

Start: The Lodge, Lincoln

Hare: Luxury Box and Hairy Krishna

Weather: Torrential downpours preceding the run, followed by a light drizzle.

Of special mention: This day marked the 65th anniversary of the day Basket’s mother said WTF is that.

Pack: Basket, Oozie, Flo Banger, Wee Balls, She Mail, Baby Butt Plug, Amish, Retard, Crotch Tiger, Rusty, Bondo, WHO.

Visitors: Just Eric from Groton CT & 1 more from CT

Socialites: Pat My Retard and 1 more

 

There were a couple of complications with tonight’s run. First of all we had had 24 hours of non stop rain and the Patriots were playing tennis at Gillette. None of that mattered as 16 of us gathered in the parking lot & discussed the niceties of Bondo brew, the only thing better than the niceties would have been nice titties.

 

We headed off in the full knowledge that the hares were still setting trail and that rain makes flour disappear rather fast. That said, She Mail exited stage left swiftly followed by Basket and Flo. We were quickly upon the first check and it was right behind the pub. However, a quick jaunt past the check revealed true trail and so it was WHO that led us up and over the rock with Retard and Wee Balls trying to keep up with his record setting pace.

 

Oozie on the other hand was munching on his apple and muttering something about the end of the world. We’re still not sure if this related to Chelsea’s recent loss or the fact that the Mayan’s had an issue in coming out with another calendar.

 

Trail brought us through minimal shaggy & trees, alas we found out later that said area was actually someone’s backyard and they were not amused. However, SheMale did a wondrous job in explaining the situation to the nice officer who in turn decided that the search for real criminals was far more rewarding than dealing with this bunch on a wet night.

 

Suddenly we were standing the edge of the abyss, a road with two lanes each going in opposite directions. One car stopped and a ghastly vision of a train wreck in motion sprang to one’s mind. Relief set in as we made it to the other side & unlike the chicken we knew why. A short distance later we found the “B” and the hares atop a rock. WHO & Bondo were sucking down the beer until others found the route and we all joked about the fact that we could see the cars from where we stood. Oozie finally made it in, but found that we had drunk all the beer a few seconds prior to his arrival, what a coincidence.

 

Hairy on the other hand, was serious when he told us trail actually went “that way” for 5 miles but then he lightened up and said naw we’re crossing the road and going back. Once again we were greeted with a run of less than 2 miles!

 

Baby Butt Plug and Just Eric led us back to the cars, a sighting of WIPOS was a negative positive, Amish said he enjoyed himself and would return next week, Crotchy promised to bake a cake for Basket next year & Basket defied gravity with his birthday balloon tied to his horn.

 

The circle had the usual run of the mill stuff, & once again Rusty got hash shit because WHO said so. We bid farewell to the Groton hashers & adjourned to the Lodge only to be greeted by a waitress that informed us of the $2.99 Harpoon beer special tonight. Popcorn was strewn about the floor and inside the glasses, Pat My retard arrived with special birthday prezzies for Basket and when it was all said and done we left.

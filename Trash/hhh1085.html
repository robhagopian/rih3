Run #1085, January 15, 2007

Hare:  Eenie Weenie Dick

Location:  Black Hut Management Area, Burrillville

Weather:  Mid 30’s, Clear

Present:  Dr WHO, Basket Boom Boom, Async, Oozing SD, Justin My Ass.

 

The Run:

I don’t remember this run, except that it was dark, the marks were invisible, and (I think) we made a counterclockwise circle, with a fair amount of bushwhacking, and the hare losing his own trail. Business as usual. The start, off North Shore Drive at Spring Lake, was to be repeated by the same hare (trying to get it right this time) in August. That one was only better because of the presence of quality bimbos. The hare has learned nothing. But he is free to add his recollections to make this an actual write-up. I’m sick of it all!

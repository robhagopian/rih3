Oozing Swims the Channel!

 
Run # 793 July 16, 2001

Hare:   Tinker & Short Shorts

Scribe:  Oozing

Where:   Wickford

The Weather:  80's, Humid

Guest Chef:Jah

Present: Async, DrWHO Slasher, WIPOS,  Trail Hoover, and Oozing SD

Absent:  the K9 management team

Well, in typical RIH3 fashion, we started promptly at 630pm, not waiting for anyone. Shortshorts(SS) pointed the pack out down Elam St and the trail was marked with enough flour to feed the guatemalan village being given refuge by the Worm's girlfriend! Across 102 and south towards Narragansett and a right up a road and soon into the woods after a check easily navigated by the pack led by Async.

Into the woods and across a wooden bridge to another check and here a classic Tinker-check baffled the pack for a few minutes before the pack headed back on the trail and to another trail that led out of the woods, past a teepee (damn Indians everywhere!), across 102 and towards the library, into the woods, out to Wilson Park and into the trail that winded around a bit but no one got lost with SS in the rear, out to the road and towards the water with Tinker waiting with a boat. [Editors note:  Yes, that was a single sentence.]

Now the water was cold and so all took to the boat and Tinker took them across, but Oozing braved the cold water and  swam across like a fish!   What a brave lad!  Across and onto Cornelius Island and trail continued by the shore and to the BC. The pack enjoyed beer and the sight of wet, naked latino beauties bathing in the water nearby AND they loved our singing! On the trail back, Oozing and trail elected to take the boat with Tinker and the rest took the short way back to Tinker's. Across the bay we sailed and towards the town and under the road and to the pond behind Tinker's. The circle was behind the house as the unanimous vote was despite absence of Bondo/Basket the run was crap! WIPOS was annointed the hashit (his staff) for his sins.  Down downs to the hares. No "see food" this time as Jah prepared delicious Thai food which was devoured by all and Oozing took care of the rice. Tinker's neighbour visited with her dogs-so well behaved it was difficult to believe with the K9s that grace our runs! A good time had by all...ONON.

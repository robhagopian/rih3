Singin' in the Rain Hash

Run #1201, April 6, 2009

Hare:  WIPOS

Location:  Grassy Plains Park, East Providence .

Weather:  40’s, Raining .

Present:   Dr WHO, Wee Balls, Donkey Punch, Pubic Housing, Oozing SD, Basket Boom Boom.

The Run:

Starting in a light rain from a small park near the border of East Providence and Borrington, the pack headed west, and soon were lost. Finally, marks were found turning northwest into a bushwhack in a nice swamp. Flour conservation had been going on, and the hare gave helpful hints. But this was not virgin territory, and WHO finally tired of the cold water alternating with briars. He turned northeast to get out to the old sand and gravel workings. The smart ones followed. Wee Balls stuck with the hare.

 

A few sodden marks were found, indicating that the pack had made the right choice. But which way to go? The group circled around the open area, occasionally coming across a mark or a check. It seemed that trail was continuing northwest. Several falsies north were found. Even worse, the hare and Wee Balls were found. Finally, true trail was followed north to the tennis club, where trail crossed the parking lot and re-entered the woods eastward to the beer check.

 

The beer was quickly found and opened. Only Basket continued on, and then even he figured out that he was only FRB because the rest were enjoying their beer in his absence. Too bad the waning daylight wasn’t enough to lose him permanently. Few songs were sung, being within range of the tennis club, and once the beer was gone, flashlights came out, and trail resumed.

 

The on-in trail led to the sides of the quarry briefly, and then headed south to a gate on Estrella Dr.  The pavement was a painful end to the run, and they quickly circled up in the field beyond the cars. Ratings for the run: too much pavement, too few bimbos, no one lost versus at least some unavoidable shiggy, fine weather, and no Bondo! Total: +0. Hashit was given to Pubic Housing in the vain hope of seeing her ring. And finishing up, they made their way to the Red Bridge Tavern to abuse the waitress and behave childishly, yet again.

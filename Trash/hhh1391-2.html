Run # 1391 - November 26th 2012<br>

Start: Wally’s Ale House, Tiverton<br>

Hare: Dr. WHO<br>

Weather: Cold<br>

Pack: SheMailMan, Basket, AssQuack, Crabby< The Butler, Rusty, Flo Banger, Oozie, Donkey Punch, Pubic Housing, Wee Balls, Fuwangi, Dog Meat<br><br>

 

WHO said, the back of the lot, and so we gathered only to be told that there was no beer and no flour. This is not a good thing to hear at 6:10pm on  Monday night! <br><br>

 

The cars rolled in and we stood around chatting as we played “lights on/lights off” with Assquack’s dog Hendrix aka Jimmy. In that little dog’s mind (nice has name for a human) he thought he was looking at UFO’s as he barked around like a fucking lunatic to little white dots on the ground. Enough with the play acting said Fuwangi, let’s get down to business here. <br><br>

 

WHO’s instructions were short, polite and to the point – “that way” he said and that way we went. Travelling through prickers and rocks, in an area that will soon become a library. Our first flour mark was an arrow some 500 yds from the start, this first mark drew verbal complaints from Donkey whereas FU appeared to be muttering under his breath. The artists community was in sight & we ran parallel to it down a nice cut walk. We were soon at the end of this manicured area and beating bush to beat the band, flour was somewhat sparse when we came to what looked like a crater in the ground but Crabby found the route with Flo and the Butler a short distance behind. <br><br>

 

Twisting and winding our way through the trees we eventually made it to the southernmost part of tonight’s trail, we then headed west along a stone wall and then came to a check that was laid by a scheming bastard. SheMail took a right and only found two marks, so he came back and followed Assquack as he had gone straight. This too was a mistake, as the pack happened upon a CB 6.9. FU was once again muttering, WHO was laughing, Donkey was taking notes so that we could all read about this later & Wee Balls really didn’t give a toss. <br><br>

 

We did an about turn and found the turd mark after the two that SheMail had previously seen, a short time later we directed into the bushes on the right whereupon we found a “BN”. Searching was fruitless as this turned out to be another smartarse move on the part of our hare, WHO had hidden the beer in the bushes on the left side of trail. DogMeat knew the smell and found the bag to everyone’s delight. A marathon singing session took place at the beer stop with golden oldies and new ditties were rolling one after the other as if we were all sitting around a camp fire for the night. <br><br>

 

Time moved on and so did we, the next trick of the evening was finishing a the run and holding the circle in an area known as “Basket Swamp”. How appropriate, was WHO trying to butter up Basket & trade said name for a piece of DogMeat? That question remains unanswered to this very day. <br><br>

 

There were no visitors, there was no WIPOS, there was no Bondo – the run received a 6.9+ & Rusty got the new hash shit as prepared by WHO. A bright redish/pinkish douche bag was awarded with funny white straws coming out of it. <br><br>

 

We made our way back to the cars & Wally’s place for some grub and a chat about that. <br>

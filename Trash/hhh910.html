Fall River Shiggy Tour

Summary for Run #910, Sept, 22, 2003

Hare: Dr WHO
Scribe: EverReady (summary courtesty of Dr Who, until the Boring Married Lady submits her purple hose..er, prose)
Location: Freetown-Fall River State Forest
Present: WIPOS, Async, Bondo, Birdbrain, Just Tina, Oozing SD, SESYB, EB Ripta (NH3) and Late Arrivals: Basket Boom Boom, Fuwangi Boner. Management: Jake, Ben, Seamus.


A brilliantly conceived run, starting north on abandoned portion of Riggenbach (Watuppa Pond) Road (except for the latecummers who were duped by a false arrow left by Oozing to confuse Basket). Into the woods for a counterclockwise loop with checkback, bike race markings, a stream crossing with a sinkhole (which RIPTA particularly enjoyed), some dirt roads to a pine forest heading west, to a stream traverse of 100yds, bushwhack to RR grade, then up to BC atop a 100ft cliff/ old quarry. A long wait for the Evil One and unfortunately the bugle sounds of Basket, and Fuwangi in Red Culottes, then, all together, on out to a forest of dead trees/toxic waste, the FR landfill, and the circle at the dead end of the starting road.
Ratings: 69!
Hashit: WHO, no Bondo.
On-After: T.K.O'Malleys on N Main, for pizza, wings, fries, nachos, Bass and Guiness. $11 a person!
RUN of the Year!

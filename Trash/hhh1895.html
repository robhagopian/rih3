For the June 20th 2022 trail we returned to Wrentham State Forest but to the less used north east corner.


Trail was rather straightforward for a WIPOS trail. We went right up the hill to an early W. We continued the ups and downs easily except for Basket who thought it would be a good idea to venture further south despite the lack of marks. We had some great vistas at the top of one of the hills and lamented the lack of W at the universally agreed better location.


After less than 2 miles your scribe arrived first at the top of Pinnacle Hill to find the B. The B was, of course, hidden far from the B - four marks down the out trail and buried under leaves. But your scribe found it and got the single Captain's Daughter tallboy so no complaints there. While there was a sheer rock face, there were no vistas at that location. Basket finally decided to rejoin trail and arrived rather late.


A quick out trail just tipped the length over the 2 mile mark. Your scribe was selected as hashit for reasons so arbitrary they were not retained.


The hashit went to HMHHH's KNURD and had a great time.
